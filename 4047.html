使用 CDPATH 变量在目录间跳转
2009-02-28 14:21:38 

In terminal, you can easily execute programs in the directories shown in PATH.


Similarly, you can easily change to directories in the directories shown in CDPATH.


Example:


if your $CDPATH is ".:~", and you have a folder "Document" in your home directory("~"), you can change your working directory to "Document" by simply typing "cd Document" in the terminal.
